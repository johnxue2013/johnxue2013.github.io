About CZE Decoration materials suppliers & manufacturers

Shandong Chengze Decoration Materials Co., Ltd. is affiliated to Linyi Yachen Wood Industry. It is a board manufacturer integrating production, sales and research and development. Linyi CZE WPC decking suppliers & manufacturers main products include WPC wallboard, WPC floor, indoor SPC floor, PVC foam board, wood-plastic floor and wood-plastic decorative materials. The company has more than 30 advanced wood equipment production lines and more than 500 skilled employees. It is only 280 kilometers away from Qingdao Port. Our factory has more than 20 years of experience in international trade, has a professional R&D team, and has the strength to develop new products. More than 20 kinds of products are exported to more than 80 countries and regions in Asia, Europe, Africa, America, Oceania, etc. Products are widely used in hotel decoration, tourism industry, and many other industries.
