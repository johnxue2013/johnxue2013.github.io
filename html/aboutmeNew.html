ABOUT US

Company Profile： Linyi Wanshijia Building Decoration Materials Co., Ltd. is located in Linyi City, Shandong Province, a famous panel production base in China. It was established in 2014 and currently has more than 60 employees. It is committed to building a world-class decorative material production base. Based on the world's leading technology and production equipment, the company implements strict material, production and customer relationship management systems, and has successively passed relevant certifications and testing. Linyi Wanshijia Building Decoration Materials Co., Ltd. focuses on providing customers with high-quality environmentally friendly panels. The product range covers hundreds of categories such as WPC, and is very popular in domestic and foreign markets.
